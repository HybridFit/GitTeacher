THE GIT GYM — Full Body Workout Plan
=====================================

Coach: Alex
Last updated: March 2026

WARM-UP (15 minutes)
- Foam rolling: 3 minutes
- Jumping jacks: 2 minutes
- High knees: 2 minutes
- Arm circles: 1 minute
- Bodyweight squats: 2 minutes
- Hip openers: 2 minutes
- Dynamic stretching: 3 minutes

MAIN WORKOUT
------------

1. Barbell Squats
   Sets: 4 | Reps: 8-10 | Rest: 90s
   Notes: Keep chest up, knees tracking over toes

2. Bench Press
   Sets: 4 | Reps: 8-10 | Rest: 90s
   Notes: Full range of motion, control the descent

3. Deadlifts
   Sets: 3 | Reps: 6-8 | Rest: 120s
   Notes: Hinge at hips, neutral spine throughout

4. Overhead Press
   Sets: 3 | Reps: 8-10 | Rest: 90s
   Notes: Brace core, no excessive back arch

5. Bent-Over Rows
   Sets: 3 | Reps: 10-12 | Rest: 60s
   Notes: Squeeze shoulder blades at top

6. Lunges
   Sets: 3 | Reps: 10 each leg | Rest: 60s
   Notes: Step far enough to keep front knee behind toes

7. Dumbbell Bicep Curls
   Sets: 3 | Reps: 12 | Rest: 45s
   Notes: No swinging, controlled movement

8. Plank Hold
   Sets: 3 | Duration: 45-60 seconds | Rest: 45s
   Notes: Straight line from head to heels

9. Bulgarian Split Squats
   Sets: 3 | Reps: 10 each leg | Rest: 60s
   Notes: Rear foot elevated on bench, keep torso upright

10. Face Pulls
    Sets: 3 | Reps: 15 | Rest: 45s
    Notes: great for shoulder health

COOL-DOWN (5 minutes)
- Hamstring stretch: 30s each side
- Quad stretch: 30s each side
- Chest doorway stretch: 30s
- Child's pose: 1 minute
- Deep breathing: 1 minute
