THE GIT GYM — Nutrition Guide
==============================

General Guidelines:
- Eat protein with every meal (aim for 1.6-2.2g per kg bodyweight)
- Stay hydrated: at least 2 liters of water daily
- Eat whole foods, minimize processed snacks
- Time your meals: eat within 2 hours after training

BREAKFAST OPTIONS
-----------------
Option A: Oatmeal with banana, honey, and walnuts
Option B: Scrambled eggs (3) with whole grain toast and avocado
Option C: Greek yogurt with granola and mixed berries

LUNCH OPTIONS
-------------
Option A: Grilled chicken breast with brown rice and steamed broccoli
Option B: Tuna salad wrap with mixed greens and olive oil dressing
Option C: Turkey and quinoa bowl with roasted vegetables

DINNER OPTIONS
--------------
Option A: Salmon fillet with sweet potato and green beans
Option B: Lean beef stir-fry with vegetables and basmati rice
Option C: Chicken pasta with tomato sauce and side salad

SNACKS
------
- Handful of mixed nuts (almonds, cashews, walnuts)
- Protein shake with milk and a banana
- Apple slices with peanut butter
- Rice cakes with cottage cheese

PRE-WORKOUT (30-60 min before)
- Banana with a tablespoon of peanut butter
- Small bowl of oatmeal

POST-WORKOUT (within 30 min)
- Protein shake
- Chicken breast with rice (if meal timing works)

Remember: nutrition is just as important as training!
